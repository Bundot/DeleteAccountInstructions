üìã Overview

Eatsy provides two options for managing your account:

  • 1. Deactivate Account - Temporarily disable your account (reversible)
  • 2. Delete Account - Permanently delete your account (30-day grace period)

Both actions require OTP (One-Time Password) verification for security.

üî∏ How to Deactivate Your Account

Deactivating your account is temporary and reversible. Your profile will be hidden, but you can reactivate it anytime by logging back in.

1 Navigate to Account Settings

  1. Open the Eatsy app
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Tap on Account Settings

2 Select Deactivate Account

  1. On the Account Settings screen, you'll see two options
  2. Tap on Deactivate Account (orange icon with pause symbol)
  3. Read the warning that explains what happens when you deactivate

  • ‚Ä¢ Your profile will be hidden

3 Provide a Reason

  1. Select a reason for deactivating:
    • Taking a break
    • Privacy concerns
    • Too many notifications
    • Not using the app
    • Other (requires additional text input)
  2. If you select "Other", type your specific reason
  3. Tap Continue

4 Verify with OTP

  1. An OTP code will be sent to your registered email
  2. Check your email for the 6-digit verification code
  3. Enter the code in the OTP input field
  4. Tap Deactivate Account

5 Account Deactivated

  1. You'll see a confirmation message: "Account Deactivated"
  2. The message confirms you can reactivate anytime by logging in
  3. Tap OK
  4. You'll be logged out automatically

üî∏ How to Delete Your Account

Deleting your account is permanent but includes a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel the deletion.

1 Navigate to Account Settings

  1. Open the Eatsy app
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Tap on Account Settings

2 Select Delete Account

  1. On the Account Settings screen, you'll see two options
  2. Tap on Delete Account (red icon with trash symbol)
  3. Read the DANGER WARNING

‚ö†Ô∏è PERMANENT ACTION

  • ‚Ä¢ All your data will be deleted
  • ‚Ä¢ This cannot be undone after 30 days
  • ‚Ä¢ You have 30 days to cancel

3 Provide a Reason

  1. Select a reason for deleting:
    • No longer need the service
    • Privacy concerns
    • Found a better alternative
    • Too expensive
    • Other (requires additional text input)
  2. If you select "Other", type your specific reason
  3. Tap Continue

4 Final Confirmation

  1. An OTP code will be sent to your registered email
  2. Check your email for the 6-digit verification code
  3. Enter the code in the Verification Code field
  4. Type "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" exactly as shown (all caps) in the confirmation field
  5. Tap Delete My Account

5 Deletion Scheduled

  1. You'll see a confirmation message: "Account Scheduled for Deletion"
  2. The message confirms your account will be deleted in 30 days
  3. You can cancel this within the grace period
  4. Tap OK
  5. You'll be logged out automatically

‚è≥ What Happens After Deletion?

  • ‚è≥ 30-day grace period begins
  • ‚úÖ You can cancel deletion within 30 days
  • ‚ùå After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted
  • ‚ùå This action cannot be undone after the grace period

üî∏ How to Reactivate a Deactivated Account

If you've deactivated your account and want to come back, reactivation is simple!

1 Access Reactivation Screen

  1. Open the Eatsy app
  2. From the login screen, look for the Reactivate Account option
  3. Tap on Reactivate Account

2 Enter Your Email

  1. You'll see a welcome message: "Welcome Back!"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Tap Send Code

3 Verify with OTP

  1. An OTP code will be sent to your email
  2. Check your email for the 6-digit verification code
  3. Enter the code in the OTP input field
  4. Tap Reactivate Account

4 Account Reactivated

  1. You'll see a confirmation message: "Welcome Back!"
  2. Your account has been reactivated successfully
  3. All your data is restored
  4. You'll be automatically logged in

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel a scheduled deletion?

Yes! You have 30 days to cancel the deletion before it becomes permanent.

What happens to my data when I deactivate?

All your data is preserved and will be restored when you reactivate.

How long does it take to deactivate/delete my account?

Deactivation is immediate. Deletion is scheduled for 30 days from the request.

Can I reactivate after deleting?

Only within the 30-day grace period. After that, deletion is permanent.

What if I don't receive the OTP code?

Check your spam folder. If still not received, try requesting a new code or contact support.

Will I lose my order history?

Deactivate: No, it's preserved. Delete: Yes, after the 30-day grace period.
